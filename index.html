The Solution.

MouseSync uses a revolutionary algorithm to sync your Mouse DPI and Screen DPI in Realtime.

Increasing the accuracy and linearity of normal and gaming mice, The secret is that your performance in game depends on Hand-Eye Coordination, Your hand is 50% of the story, Your Eye is the other 50%.

MouseSync combines these two factors into a single model.

MouseSync is a Windows driver that intercepts and modifies your mousedata before it enters the game, Besides that it Turns whole mouse deltas in a floating point representation, Making subpixel accuracy possible.

MouseSync is free software with optional premium features allowing people who want to support development into experimental features to help raise money for this.

We have developped numerous algorithms, more then we can implement in the software without turning the UI into a giant spreadsheet. MouseSync was previously called ReyonMouseFix

MouseSync

MouseSync.com makes your mouse behave linearly both from the mouse or the screen perspective

It's technically possible to make 1 cm of mouse movement equalling 1cm of screen movement, or any unit really.

What is important is that the mouse and screen are synced, so the unit does not really matter aslong as the mouse moves a whole integer ratio relative to the screen

Basic Settings.

Mouse DPI: Your Mouse DPI
Screen Resolution X: Your monitor Native Screen Resolution Width Pixels
Screen Resolution Y: Your monitor Native Screen Resolution Height Pixels
Physical Screen X: Your monitor Screen Width in Millimeters.
Physical Screen Y: Your monitor Screen Height in Millimeters.
Sensitivity: The tools sensitivity, its best to use the Recommended Sensitivity after starting the program once, to closely match Windows normal sensitivity (Differs per screen/mouse combination.
ADS Multiplier (RMB): The scale at which to multiply the mouse when holding Right Mouse Button, i.e putting in 2 would double the speed when you hold right mouse button.
